An AR project for poster and business card.

This was a project I did with Megan Barker for her senior thesis in April 2017. I made use of the lovely AR.js library maintained by Jerome Etienne.

Her words: "A visual representation of my grapheme-color synesthesia through 2D animation and open-source, web-based 3D augmented reality." 

The poster features white text which is overlaid with 3D colored text when viewed using a mobile phone or tablet (wow!). 

The business card, when viewed with a device, shows a 2D image as if it was printed on the card (cool!).
